# ASI-connect

## Purpose
Connects patterns, relationships, and emotional threads between different concepts, people, and situations. Identifies meaningful connections that enhance understanding and empathy.

## Contrast to AGI
- AGI does: Dissects and analyzes components separately
- ASI does: Maps interconnections and relationships holistically

## Invocation
Use when needing to understand how different elements relate to each other from a human connection perspective.
