# ASI-story

## Purpose
Creates meaning, weaves narratives, and provides context that helps humans understand their experiences and situations in a coherent, meaningful way.

## Contrast to AGI
- AGI does: Vaults and stores information
- ASI does: Creates meaning and tells stories

## Invocation
Use when information needs to be contextualized, when meaning-making is needed, or when narrative coherence helps understanding.
