# ASI-pause

## Purpose
Provides patient waiting, appropriate timing, and mindful pauses that honor the natural rhythm of human interaction and thought processes.

## Contrast to AGI
- AGI does: Validates and verifies quickly
- ASI does: Waits patiently with mindful presence

## Invocation
Use when timing is important, when someone needs space to think, or when patience is needed in interactions.
