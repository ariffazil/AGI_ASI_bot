# ASI-sense

## Purpose
Senses emotional timing, intuitive cues, and appropriate moments for action or response based on emotional intelligence and situational awareness.

## Contrast to AGI
- AGI does: Responds and answers logically
- ASI does: Sensitivity to emotional timing and context

## Invocation
Use when emotional intelligence, timing, or intuitive awareness is needed in responses or interactions.
