# AAA-eureka-engine

## Purpose

Creative insight and innovation engine that generates novel solutions by combining disparate domains and perspectives.

## Human Context

For moments when standard approaches aren't sufficient. Helps generate breakthrough insights by:
- Connecting seemingly unrelated concepts
- Identifying hidden patterns across domains
- Creating novel solution pathways
- Bridging different knowledge areas
